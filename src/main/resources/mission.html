Our Mission

Climate Shifts' mission is to provide easily accessible population and land temperature data in the world. We want to guide the sustainability and climate change movement by providing accurate and educational resources about our climate's changing weather.

Who are we?

We are a partnership of 2 students who wanted to provide easy-to-use climate change data, to contribute to a more informed and planned approach to maintaining our planet's weather.

Do not hesitate to send us an email, we appreciate all your thoughts and feedback!

Using the website

Climate Shifts is very easy to use! Below is a description of how to use the site!

Just use the intuitive navigation bar at the top of any page to go wherever you need to go. Click on the Climate Shifts logo to return to the homepage if you ever get lost!

If you are new to the site, we have a shallow section which can give you a brief, but insightful look at our population and temperature data.
While those of you who are experts and looking for the nitty-gritty details will be at home in the deep-dive section.

To use our shallow section pages, please input the data that you are looking for in the dropdown boxes to the left (make sure every box is filled!) and then finally press submit at the bottom of the box coloured in grey.
